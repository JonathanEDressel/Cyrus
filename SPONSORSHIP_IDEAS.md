## Affiliate Networks (Aggregated Discovery)

- **[Impact.com](https://impact.com)** — many fintech/crypto companies list programs here; faster than applying direct.
- **[PartnerStack](https://partnerstack.com)** — SaaS and fintech focused, easy to browse relevant programs.

---

## Tips

- Have a **landing page or website** ready when applying — some programs require a web presence to review desktop apps.
- **Kraken and Coinbase** are the strongest first applications since you already have technical integration — use that as your pitch.
- Start with the **affiliate networks** (Impact, PartnerStack) to find additional programs in one place.
